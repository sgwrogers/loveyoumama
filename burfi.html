Burfi

Chantal
29 July 2021

Ingredients


  • 1/4 mug water
  • 1 mug sugar
  • 6-7 oz. ground almonds
  • 1tbsp milk powder
  • 6-7pods cardamom seeds powdered

Method


Boil sugar and water until solution is of ‘one string consistency’


Add milk powder and almonds and stir briskly for 5 minutes making sure it does not burn.


Add cardamom powder and spread flat onto a tray.


After it has completely cooled cut into squares.
